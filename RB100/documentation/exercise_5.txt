# exercise_5.rb

https://ruby-doc.org/core-2.7.1/doc/syntax/literals_rdoc.html#label-Numbers

You can write integers of any size as follows:

1234
1_234
100_000_000
12_345

These numbers have the same value, 1,234. The underscore may be
used to enhance readability for humans. You may place an
underscore anywhere in the number.
